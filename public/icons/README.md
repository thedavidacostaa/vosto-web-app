# App Icons

Drop two square PNG images here for the app to use as its home-screen icon:

| Filename | Size |
|----------|------|
| `icon-192.png` | 192×192px |
| `icon-512.png` | 512×512px |

Use your VOSTO logo on a black or transparent background. Keep the logo centered with a little breathing room around the edges — phones crop app icons into circles or rounded squares, and anything too close to the edge gets cut off.

If you don't have these sized yet, you can resize your logo for free at **[resizeimage.net](https://resizeimage.net)** or in any photo editor — just export as PNG at both sizes above.

Until these are added, the app will still work fine; it just won't have a custom icon when added to a home screen.
